Our Favourite Green Podcasts to Enjoy on Your Commute

What exactly does it mean to be Carbon Neutral? The Carbon Neutral Podcast is one of our favourites for exploring this question from a social and economic standpoint. Another great podcast to listen to around your time with us is The Net Zero Life, where you can learn the ins and outs of sustainable living from climate enthusiast Nathan Zvi.

At CodeNode, we host many events which explore technology. We love building our commercial awareness with The Greener Tech Podcast, a series of candid talks with professionals on topics from rural mobility to ethical branding.

We carefully select the most efficient water systems at CodeNode to avoid unnecessary waste. (don’t) Waste Water! is one of our favourite podcasts for exploring the importance of saving water, and is a great listen-to for any commuter to our events.
