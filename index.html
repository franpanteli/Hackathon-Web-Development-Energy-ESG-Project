Carbon Hackers in partnership with CodeNode!

CodeNode was founded in 2009 by Ryan Dahl, when carbon was at a level of 388ppm. Today, this level is 412ppm, which is why we believe it’s important to consider how we take climate action as individuals.

CodeNode is steadfastly dedicated to realizing their ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) objectives, with a strong emphasis on diminishing their environmental footprint. Recognizing the urgency of environmental challenges, they are committed to implementing sustainable practices that actively reduce their impact on the planet.

Through meticulous planning and innovative strategies, CodeNode is spearheading efforts to foster a greener future, ensuring that their operations align with the principles of sustainability. By prioritizing environmental stewardship, CodeNode exemplifies their unwavering commitment to creating a more sustainable world.
